The Wonderboard - Case Study

This project challenges the current state of online education and how it adapts students from the traditional in-classroom educational experience to an online educational curriculum, which was caused largely by the viral spread of covid-19, and attempts to facilitate said transition by adapting the educational workspace (the application User Interface) to suit the users (students) needs and support them in their educational pursuits.

DHEN Gallery Webfront Redesign

I was contacted by the owner of DHEN gallery, a vintage collection, to redesign their online storefront. During a later discussion at the gallery, it was determined that I would be the sole designer in charge of the project. Having accepted this job, I was left to determine the ultimate goal of the redesign project, perform research on related matters, and choose a direction in which to take the website. Aside from this, I would also do photo editing and mockups for the owner when asked to.
